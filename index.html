Welcome

Allamah Institute of Higher Education

The ALLAMAH University (AU) is a private university and one of the highly qualified universities that provide academic and research services in the country. ALLAMAH University was established on the basis of philosophy that only by spreading knowledge and upgrading awareness one can overcome people’s problems. ALLAMAH University as a legal entity prepares its study programs according to the rules and regulations Islamic Republic of Afghanistan and the Ministry of Higher Education. ...

Vision of Allamah University

achieving a top position in similar institutions relying on valid scientific standards, educational services and social trust.

Mission of Allamah University

Training Committed & Expoert human Resources in order to develop scientific programs & educational service in accordance with national & international standards in the country.
